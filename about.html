Our Project Goal

As part of our MIS 201 course, the "Nice Car" project aims to apply key information systems concepts to design and develop a functional prototype for an online car dealership based in Riyadh. We focused on creating a user-friendly interface, structuring product information effectively, and understanding the basics of e-commerce website development. This platform showcases our combined skills in software, networking, and business strategy.
